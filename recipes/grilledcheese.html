Seasoned Grilled Cheese!

A quick simple snack that takes less than five minutes!


  • Two slices of any bread
  • Sliced deli cheese
  • Some spices
  • Deli sliced meats optional
    •
    1. lightly grease skillet with oil and spices
    2. add cheese and meats(optional) to the bread
    3. Place sandwich in skillet
    4. Flip when cheese starts to melt, wait about 45 seconds until bread is golden brown.
