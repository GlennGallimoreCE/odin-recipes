Feta Hamburger!

A stuffed meat burger with feta cheese, you could always substitute the feta for another type of cheese.


  • Buns
  • MEAT! Grounded
  • Feta Cheese
  • Veggies: Lettuce, Onion, Tomatoes
    •
    1. Fire up the grill!
    2. season the meat, stuff it with the feta cheese
    3. Place stuffed patties on the grill
    4. chop up any veggies: lettuce, onions, tomatoes
