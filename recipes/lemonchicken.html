Lemony Chicken!

Impress your wine snob neighbors with a fancy meal!


  • 1/4 olive oil
  • 1/2 all purpose flour
  • 1 large lemon, wedges cut
  • 6 pieces of boneless, skinless chicken breast
    •
    1. Heat olive oil in skillet over med-high heat
    2. Place flour in shallow bowl, season the chicken breast then gently press into flour to coat.
    3. Add chicken to skillet, cook until golden brown. Optional: add white wine and/or lemon juice.
    4. Keep cooking until chicken is no longer pink in the center and juices have reduced by half.
    5. Remove the skillet from heat, then stir in butter into the sauce until fully melted. Pour said sauce over the chicken then garnish with lemon wedges.
